====== Characteristics ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

=== Characteristics(Things that help you identify rocks) of rocks are : ===
	* **Color** - the color of the rock
	* **Texture** - how the rock feels
	* **Weight** - how heavy the rock is
	* **Hardness** - how hard the rock is
	* **Density** - how dense the rock is
