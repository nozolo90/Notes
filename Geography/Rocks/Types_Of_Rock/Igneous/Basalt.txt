====== Basalt ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

**Basalt** is a heavy , black rock formed when volcanic lava **cooled very quickly**

=== Characteristics ===
	[[Igneous]] Rock
	Heavy and Black
	it is **hexagonally shaped**

=== Found in ===
	it is found in the **giants causeway**
