====== Granite ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

**Granite** is a hard , coarse rock formed when magma cooled slowly deep within the earths crust.

=== Characteristics ===
	**Hard** and **Coarse**
	Has visible colored **crystals**(feldspar **quarts** and **meca**)


=== Found in ===
	**Wicklow Mountains**

=== Uses ===
	Building
	Monuments
