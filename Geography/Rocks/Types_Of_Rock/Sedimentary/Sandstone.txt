====== Sandstone ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

**Sandstone** is formed when layers of sand at the bottom of the ocean or in the desserts built up, compressing the previous layers into solid rock

=== Characteristics ===
	* [[Sedimentary]] Rock
	* red/brown color
	* coarse

=== Found in ===
	* Macgillycuddy reek

=== Uses ===
	* Building house walls
