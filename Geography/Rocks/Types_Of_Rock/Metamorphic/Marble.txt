====== Marble ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

**Marble** is formed from [[Types Of Rock:Sedimentary:Limestone|Limestone]]

=== Characteristics ===
	* [[Metamorphic]] rock
	* Hard rock
	* varies in color depending on where it is mined
		* **red ( Cork )**
		* **black ( Killkeny )**

=== Uses ===
	* Mantle Pieces
