====== Sedimentary ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

**Sedimentary Rocks** are formed when layers of **sediment** pile up on top of each other, **compressing the lower layers into rock**

=== The Sedimentary Rocks are ===
	* [[+Limestone|Limestone]]
	* [[+Sandstone|Sandstone]]
	* [[+Shale|Shale]]
	* [[+Coal|Coal]]
