====== Types Of Slope ======


Created Monday 27 November 2017

=== Even ===
* These slopes have evenly spaced contours

=== Convex ===
* these slopes are steep at the start, then flater nearer to the end

=== Concave ===
* these maps are flater near the start, but steeper near the end

=== Compound ===
* These slopes are both concave and convex combined
