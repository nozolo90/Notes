====== Altitude ======


Created Monday 27 November 2017

* __Triangulation Stations__ show the highths of some mountain peaks
* __Contours__ are curved lines that show areas of equal height
* On most maps __color__ is used to indicate altitude. lower areas are shown in green and higher areas are show in brown
* __Spot heights__ show the heights of some areas between contours
