====== Migration ======
Created Thursday 03 May 2018

Migration is when people move from one place to another.

**Internal migration **is migration within a country.
**Emmigration** is when people move out of a country.
**Immigration** is when people move into a country.
**Push factors** are things that make you want to emmigrate - pushes you away.
* Like persecution, bad weather, no jobs, dull social life, no schools
**Pull factors** are things that make you want to emmigrate into a certain country - attractive features.
* Good weather, good jobs, good education, good shcool system.
**Barriers to migration **are things that prevent migration.
* Leaving familly, no VISA
