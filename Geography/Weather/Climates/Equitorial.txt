====== Equitorial ======


Created Wednesday 06 December 2017

=== Temperature ===
* Hot all year (**32 degrees celcius**).

=== Precipitaion ===
* Rain every afternoon - mostly convectional.
* High level of humidity.
* **2000ml** of rain per year.

=== Biodiversity ===
* Exotic birds, monkeys, parrots
