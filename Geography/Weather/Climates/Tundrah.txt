====== Tundrah ======


Created Thursday 07 December 2017

=== Temperature ===
* They experience **short**, **cool summers** avereging around **5 degrees celcius**.
* They experience **long and cold winters **averaging around **-35 degrees celcius**.
* There is a large **temperature range**.

=== Precipitation ===
* 250 mil per year.

=== Vegetation ===
* Little vegetation can survive in the harsh conditions. mostly mosses and litchens.
* Animals and birds are present during the summer but they migrate in the winter.
