====== Aspect ======


Created Sunday 03 December 2017

=== Statement ===
* Aspect is the direction a slope is facing.

=== Development ===
* In the northern hemishphere south facing slpes are warmer.
* This is because the sun rays shine directly on to them.
* They are also warmer because they are protected from cold northerly winds by the slope
