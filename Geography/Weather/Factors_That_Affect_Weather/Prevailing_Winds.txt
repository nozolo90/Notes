====== Prevailing Winds ======


Created Sunday 03 December 2017

=== Statement ===
* Prevailing winds are the winds that blow most often over an area.
* In ireland, we have south westerly prevailing winds.
* Prevailing winds can make an area **hotter, colder, wetter or drier**.
