====== Rain types ======
Created Monday 23 April 2018

=== Convection ===
* The sun **heats the ground**.
* The **ground heats air**, and the **warmed air rises**.
* As the warm air **rises**, it **cools and condeses** forming **cumulos clouds**.
* Cumulous clouds are asccociated with **thunder** and **heavy showers**.

=== Frontal ===
* A warm air mass meets a cold air mass.
* The war air mass rises over the cold air mass.
* As the warm air mass rises, it cools and condenses forming stratus clouds.
* Further condenstation causes precipitation.
