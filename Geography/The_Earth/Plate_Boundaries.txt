====== Plate Boundaries ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

**Plate Boundaries** are where plates meet

=== Transform ===
* At these **Plate Boundaries** two [[The Earth:Plates|Plates]] slide past each other
* [[The Earth:EarthQuakes|Earthquakes]] may occur

=== Constructive ===
* At these **Plate Boundaries **plates **separate**
* [[The Earth:Volcanoes|Volcanoes]] and [[The Earth:Mid Ocean Ridges|Mid ocean ridges]] can occur here

=== Destructive ===
* At these **Plate Boundaries** [[The Earth:Plates|Plates]] collide
* [[The Earth:Fold Mountains|Fold Mountains]] and ocean trenches can occur here
