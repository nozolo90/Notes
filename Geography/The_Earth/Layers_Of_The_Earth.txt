====== Layers Of The Earth ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

There are four layers of the earth

=== Crust ===
* **The Crust **is the outmost layer of the earth
* It is composed of Solid [[Geography:Rocks|Rock]]
* The crust is split into parts like an orange peel called [[Geography:The Earth:Plates|Plates]]

=== Mantle ===
* **The Mantle** is the second layer under the crust
* It is composed of slowly flowing **semi-molten **Rock

=== Outer Core ===
* The **Outer Core **is the third layer down
* It is located below the Mantle
* It is //extremely //hot and molten

=== Inner Core ===
* This Layer is at the **Center **of the earth
* The **Inner Core** is **EXTREMELY **hot, but unlike the outer core it is solid due to the immense pressure its under
* Its composed of iron and nickel
