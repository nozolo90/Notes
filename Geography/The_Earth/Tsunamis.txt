====== Tsunamis ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

When An [[EarthQuakes|Earthquake]] happens underwater, The water is disturbed causing massive waves called **Tsunamis**
The **Tsunamis **get bigger as they get closer to land
They can reach **30 meters high**

=== Effects ===
	* Tsunamis can destroy entire coastal towns
	* The water might contain diseases and bacteria
	* They cost the cities to repair damage
