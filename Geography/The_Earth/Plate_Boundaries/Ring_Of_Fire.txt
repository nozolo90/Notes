====== Ring Of Fire ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

**The Pacific Ring Of Fire** is where most of the [[The Earth:World Plates|7 major plates]] meet
The long line of [[Plate Boundaries]] means that earthquakes, tsunamis and volcanoes are common here
