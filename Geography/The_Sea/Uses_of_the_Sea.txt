====== Uses of the Sea ======
Created Friday 18 August 2017

=== Recreation ===
* beaches are a great place to **relax**. 
* we can do **water sports** in the sea too like **fishing**, **sailing** and **surfing**

=== Transport ===
* **large boats** carrying **goods** over large distances like **oil**, and **containers**.
* **Ferries** also transport people cars and trucks.

=== Food supply ===
* **Fish** provide food. Fish farms located in sheltered bays, grow a wide variety of fish.
* The fish are processed offshore, creating **jobs**
