====== Uses of the Sea ======
Created Friday 18 August 2017

=== Recreation ===
* Beaches are a great place for people to **relax**. 
* We use the sea for recreational water sports like **fishing**, **sailing** and **surfing** too.

=== Transport ===
* **Large boats** carrying **goods** over large distances like **oil**, and **containers** to ports via the sea.
* **Ferries **transport people cars and trucks to other countries.

=== Food supply ===
* **Fish** provide food. Fish farms located in sheltered bays grow a wide variety of fish.
* The fish are processed offshore, creating **jobs**
