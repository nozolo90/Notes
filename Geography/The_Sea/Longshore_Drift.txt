====== Longshore Drift ======

Created Friday 18 August 2017

=== Statement ===
	* **Longshore drift** is the movement of material across the coast due to [[Waves]]

=== Development ===
	* Waves approach at an **angle**
	* The Swash moves material **up and along** the shore
	* The Backwash moves material **down**
	* This process is repeated and the material in moved along in a **zigzag pattern**
