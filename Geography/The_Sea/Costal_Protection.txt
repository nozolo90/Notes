====== Costal Protection ======
Created Friday 18 August 2017

==== Sea Walls ====
* **concrete** **walls** **with a curve** at the top of them.
* they help **deflect** waves.
* They can be destroyed in **severe storms**.

==== Boulders ====
* large boulders placed along the coast to weaken the strength of the waves before they hit the town

==== Groynes ====
* **low walls** built of wood
* they reduce longshore drift by trapping sand.
* an example is **Rosslare**

==== Gabions ====
* Wire cages filled with small stones placed in front of beaches or small sand dunes
* they reduce the power of the waves.

All of these exept the **groynes** can be found at **Tramore**
