====== Sand Dunes ======

Created Friday 18 August 2017

=== Statement ===
	* **Sand Dunes **are hills of sand which form above the high tide level.

=== Development ===
	* Wind dries the sand on the beach making it **lighter**
	* The **wind blows the sand inland** and it is trapped by **walls** or **vegetation**
	* The sand builds up into **bigger hills** which are sand dunes
	* **Marram grass** is used to **bind the sand together** and keep it from blowing everywhere

=== Example ===
	* An example is **Tramore Co.Waterford**
