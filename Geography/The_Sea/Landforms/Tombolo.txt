====== Tombolo ======

Created Friday 18 August 2017

=== Statement ===
	* A **Tombolo **is a ridge of **sand **and **shingle **that connects an island to the mainland

=== Development ===
	* It forms when a [[Sand Spit]] grows outward from the mainland
	* Material is **Deposited in the sheltered water** between the mainland and the island

=== Example ===
	* An example is **Howth Co.Dublin**
