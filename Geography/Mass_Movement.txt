====== Mass Movement ======
Created Saturday 19 August 2017

Study for **Mass Movement**


**Mass Movement **is the movement of **regolith **downslope under the influence of gravity

=== Mass Movement Factors: ===
	* **Gradient** ( steepness of the slope )
	* **Water** **content** - the amount of water in **regolith** - increases weight
	* **Human** **Activity** - people dig into hillsides and other land, increasing the **gradient**
	* **Vegetation** - **plants** hold soil together and removes water, slowing down **mass movement**
