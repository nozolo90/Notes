====== Stalagmites ======

Created Saturday 19 August 2017

=== Statement ===
	* **Stalagmites** are usually formed under [[Stalactites]].

=== Development ===
	* When drops of water containing **disolved specs of calcite ( **[[Geography:Rocks:Types Of Rock:Sedimentary:Limestone|limestone]] ) fall on the cave floor from the **tip of a Stalactite** ,  they evapourate leaving some **specs of calcite** of the floor.
	* Over thousands of years , **theese specs build up to form stalagmites**
