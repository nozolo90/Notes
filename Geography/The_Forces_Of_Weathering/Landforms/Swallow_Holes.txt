====== Swallow Holes ======

Created Saturday 19 August 2017

**Swallow Holes **are holes wher a river appears to dissapear into the ground

* when a river enters a [[Karst area]] it enters the [[Grykes]] and disapears underground.
* It slowly erodes the grykes making them into large vertical hole
* The river disapears into this hole and into a cave or something
