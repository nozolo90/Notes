====== Freeze Thaw Action ======

Created Friday 18 August 2017

**Freeze thaw** is a type of **Mechanical Weathering**

* Water **seeps into cracks** of rock in the day
* Water **freezes in the night, **expanding and putting strain on the rock and making the crack bigger
* This process continues and cracks rocks into smaller pieces called [[+Scree|scree]].

	{{~/Documents/FreezeThaw.png}}

**Freeze Thaw **occurs in the high mountains
an example is **The Alps**
