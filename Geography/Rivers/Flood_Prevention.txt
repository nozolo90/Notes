====== Flood Prevention ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

To **prevent rivers from flooding** and **damaging land**,**property** and **towns **we can do these things

	* Put up **flood barriers**
	* **Rubbish** and **vegetation** can be cleared to improve flow
	* **More trees** could be planted to **absorb rainwater**
