====== River Transportation ======


Created Saturday 19 August 2017

Material [[Erosion|eroded]] by the river **Transported** downstream
Material being carried in the river can be [[Deposition|Deposited]] in the [[Stages of The River:Old Stage|Old]] or [[Stages of The River:Mature Stage|Mature]] stage

=== There are four ways material can be transported: ===

== Rolling ==
* **Large Rocks **are pushed along the river bed by the force of the water

== Bouncing ==
* **Smaller Rocks **bounce along the riverbed , pushed by the force of the water

== Suspension ==
* **Small and Light **rocks are suspended above the riverbed and float along with the river

== Solution ==
* **Disolved Materials **are dissolved... in the river and move with it
