====== Uses Of Rivers ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

==== Hydro Electric Power ====
	* Dams are built on a river - can now be controlled
	* they release the water to turn large turbines which generate energy
	* eg //Polaphuca// & //Blessington// lake
	**advantages**
	* clean - renewable energy
	* reservoirs like **Polaphuca** provide water to Dublin
	* reservoirs offer amenities ( water sports )
	**disadvantages**
	* They can flood valuable farmland
	* salmon and other marine life could be threatened
	* its difficult and expensive to dam a river
