====== Deltas ======


Created Saturday 19 August 2017

This landform forms in the [[Rivers:Stages of The River:Old Stage|Old Stage]] at the Mouth71
**Triangle shaped piece of land which is formed at the mouth of the river**

=== How they form ===
	* As the river enter the sea it looses speed and drops off all of its remaining load
	* This material builds up to form a new **land** **delta**
	* If the **deltas** clog up the Mouth, The river breaks into smaller rivers called [[+Distributaries|Distributaries]]
