====== Ox-Bow Lake ======


Created Saturday 19 August 2017

**Ox-Bow Lakes** are lakes that were once a [[Meanders]]

=== How they're formed ===
	* They occur when continued [[Rivers:Erosion|erosion]] and [[Rivers:Deposition|deposition]] create very pronounced meanders
	* Eventually the river cuts through the neck of the meander
	* Deposition the occurs which leaves the meander disconnected from the river
