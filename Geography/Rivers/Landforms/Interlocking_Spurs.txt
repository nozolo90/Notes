====== Interlocking Spurs ======


Created Saturday 19 August 2017

This landform is found in the [[Rivers:Stages of The River:Youthful Stage|Youthful Stage]]

=== How they're formed ===
	* **Interlocking Spurs** occur when a river meets hard or resilient rock it can not [[Rivers:Erosion|erode]]
	* Instead of **eroding** it it goes around them and a zigzag pattern develops
An example is **The Youthful Stage Of The Liffey**
