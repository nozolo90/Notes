====== Peat ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017


**Peat **is partially decomposed remains of **dead** **plants** that piled up on each other over time similar to the formation on [[Geography:Rocks:Types Of Rock:Sedimentary|Sedimentary]] Rock

Peat is found in [[+Bogs|Bogs]]

=== Uses ===
	**Heating**
	**Fertilizer**
