====== Geothermal Energy ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

Water is pumped deep into the earths Crust
The deeper you get into the earths crust, the hotter it gets due to i t being closer to the Mantle

The water is heated and pumped back up where the steam is used to heat homes and turn turbines
