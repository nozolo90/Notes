====== 1798 Revolution in Ireland ======
Created Wednesday 25 April 2018

=== Causes of irish revolution rev ===
* Irish not allowed to practice their religion.
* Catholic mass made illegal.
* Killing of the united irishmen.
* Protestant ascendancy owning most good land.
* Unfair parliament - only church of irish people allowed in.

=== Consequences of irish revolution rev ===
* Punishment for irish - act of union.
* Death of UI.
* Greater tension.
* Wolfe tonne died.
