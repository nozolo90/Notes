====== Tools ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

Mesolithic people made their tools out of **flint**
**Flint **is a hard rock that splits into sharp pieces when it's broken
**Flint** is plentiful along the north coast so that would explain why people settled there.

=== What tools did they make? ===
	axes, spears, knives and scrapers
