====== Christopher Columbus Essay ======


Created Wednesday 29 November 2017

== Who was he ==
* Christopher Columbus was born in **1451** in **Genoa, Italy**.
* Genoa had a **port** so he became interested in **sailing**.

== What he believed in ==
* Columbus believed that the world was **round**.
* He thought that if he kept going **west** he would end up in **the east**.
* The east was important because they were the only places where **valuable spices** could be found.
* Spices were found in **india**, the **spice islands**. ect
* However, the continent of **America**, which no european had ever heard of was in the way.
* Columbus would have to sail around the **south of america** to get to the east, but he did not do this.

== Ships and Crew ==
* Columbus spent **8 years** trying to get a **ship** to sail and **crew** to journey with him.
* **England** did not believe in him and thought what he was doing was wrong.
* **Portugal** was content with their own route to the east.
* Eventually, he convinced spain to give him **3 ships** and **90 crew**.
* The three ships were the **nina**, **pinta** and the **santa maria**.

== Seting off ==
* They set off from the **port of palos **in spain on the **3rd of August 1492**.
* They stopped off at the **canary islands** for food and water supplies and then set off for the east.

== Journey ==
* After weeks on the ship, **the crew were getting worried**.
* To prevent mutiny, Columbus had promised his crew that they should be in **Aisia** by **four weeks**.
* Mutiny is when the crew betray the captain.
* Columbus kept **two diaries**.
* In one of them he said how **close** they were to aisia and how great everything was going
* In the other he expressed his real **concerns**
This was so that the crew wouldn't be worried.

== Land ==
* Soon, they saw **branches with berries** on them in the ocean.
* This meant that they were in **shallow waters** and that thew were near land.
* Eventually **Rodrigo Triana**, an crew member spotted land.

== Not india ==
* Columbus had reached **Central America** , not India.
* He was in what we now call **the bahamas** - Columbus named it **San salvador**
* He claimed it for spain

== Natives ==
* Columbus called the natives indians because that is were they thought that they were.
* He viseted **Hati** and the **Dominican Republic**.
* He found that the natives had **gold**, but **no spices**. This is because he was not in india.
* He left **40** **men** **on the island** and left for spain

== Kill Them All ==
* He was told to **return** to the bahamas **for more gold** by the king and queen of spain
* Upon arrival, he realised that the men he left of the island had been **killed**
* He tortured the natives and forced them to look for gold
* When the king and queen of spain heard of this, the threw him in jail.
* He was allowed one more trip to central america before he died in 1506.
