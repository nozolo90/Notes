====== Knights ======


Created Wednesday 23 August 2017

==== Jobs of a knight ====
* Maintaining **law** and **order**
* **protecting** **community** and land from attackers
* **fights** and **attacks** for the king
* [[+Entertainment|Entertainment]]

==== Who can become a knight ====
* Only the sons of **lords**/**nobles**

==== Fostrage ====
* Knights beginning their training would be sent to another knight/lord to be trained
* This was called **Fostarage**

==== Stages in becoming a knight ====

== 1. Page ==
* at the age **7** he learned how to ride a **horse,** use a blunt sword 
* and **good** **manners**

== 2. Squire ==
* At the age of **14 **he learned how to **fight** **on** **a** **horse**
* He went with his lord to help him with his weapons at war

== 3. Dubbing ==
* There would be a dubbing ceremony where the squire would become a knight
* He would spend the previous knight praying to **God**.
* At the ceremony the lord would tap his two shoulders with a sword and say " **arise** **sir** **knight**"

=== Clothes worn ===
	* **Red** - willing to bleed
	* **White** - to show you only have one lord
	* **Black** - willing to die for your lord


=== Code of chivalry ===
* A code to protect the **women** and **children**
