====== Slaves ======


Created Tuesday 22 August 2017

slaves belonged to **masters** and had **no rights**

made up **one third** of the population

They were usually **criminals** , captured enemy **citizens** or people **sold into slavery**

**Slaves** received no pay and had no say in what they did

they fought as gladiators and raced chariots

Some were **educated** and worked as **teachers** and **doctors** or **tutors**

freed slaves were called **freemen**
