====== Cena ======


Created Tuesday 22 August 2017

The **Cena** was and evening 3 course meal

=== starters ===
	**savoury** **dishes** like **raw** or **cooked** **veg**


=== main course ===
	**roast** or **boiled** meat **covered** is **rich** **sauce** to hide its not so freshness


=== dessert ===
	**pastries**, **fruit** and **nuts**
