====== Chariot Racing ======


Created Tuesday 22 August 2017

**chariot** **racing** was where teams of **chariot** **racers** and **horses** would race around a track.

The sport was **very** **dangerous**, with falls and crashes happening often

The sport was very **entertaining** for the romans
