====== Vasco De Gama ======
Created Monday 25 September 2017

=== Journey 1497 (1460 - 1524) ===
* **Used maps** drawn by diaz to reach india
* **Avoided storms** by sailing into the atlantic - in doing so he **lost sight of land**
* rounded cape on **christmas** and named it **NATAL**
* He was helped by **forgien rulers**
* Came back with **valuable spices**

De gama was very important because he had discovered a route to india via sea, avoiding the violent overland route
