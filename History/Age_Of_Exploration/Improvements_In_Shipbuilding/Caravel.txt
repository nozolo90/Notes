====== Caravel ======
Created Monday 25 September 2017

Caravel ships were a new type of ship

=== Features ===
* **Small** and **easy to steer**
* Triangular lateen sails allowed ships to **sail into the wind**
* **Strong** clinker built design
* **Water-Tight** decks
