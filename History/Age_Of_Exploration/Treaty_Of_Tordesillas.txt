====== Treaty Of Tordesillas ======
Created Monday 25 September 2017

After mapmaker amerigo discovered that the place christopher columbus discovered was a new landmass, there was a dispute over who should have control over the land.
Spain and Portugal asked the pope, so he drew a random line across the map in the atlantic.
The right side belonged to Portugal and The left side belonged to Spain.
