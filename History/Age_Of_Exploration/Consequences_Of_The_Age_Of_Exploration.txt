====== Consequences Of The Age Of Exploration ======
Created Saturday 02 December 2017

=== Explorers ===
* New Goods like gold, pineapple.
* New land
* Able to build empires
* More slaves
* Spread Christianity
* Spread language

=== Natives ===
* Disease
* Lost control of land
* Tortured
* Became Slaves
* Lost their belongings
* Were killed
* Lost religion and language
