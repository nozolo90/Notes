====== Carbon 14 Dating ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

We measure how much **Carbon 14 **is in organic matter to see how old it is

=== Technique ===
	**Carbon 14** is a chemical that natural occurs in living things.
	When a living thing dies, its Carbon 14 levels slowly deplete.
	by looking at how much Carbon 14 is in a plant/ animal, we can see how long its been dead
