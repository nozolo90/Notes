====== Wattle and Daub ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

=== Wattle and daub was a technique used to make houses ===
* **wattle** - sticks woven together to make the house frame
* **daub** - a mixture of **poop**, **straw** and **mud** that was put into the woven frame for insulation
