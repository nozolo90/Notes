====== Ulster Plantation ======


Created Sunday 03 December 2017

| Year | Organiser    | Success |

=== Why it happened ===
* Ulster was the last province of ireland not controlled **fully** by the english.
* The english were trying to impose their culture(common law,arable farming,english language) onto ulster and **the people had had enough**.
* They **rebelled** against the english in **1594**.
* It was led by **Hugh O'Neil**, earl of tyrone.
* This was the begining of the **nine years war**.

=== Nine Years War ===
* The irish lost the war
__Some important battles__

== Battle of yellow ford ==
* he irish **won** this battle, inspiring them to fight harder

== Battle of kinsale ==
	* /The irish had asked king philip of Spain to help them fight for the Catholic faith in Ulster.
	* In **1601**, The spaish army arrived in kinsale down south.
	* The Irish forces had to march down to meet up with the spainards but met english forces first.
	* The enlgish **destroyed** both armies.

== War Lost ==
* In **1603** the treaty of melifont was signed, ending the war.
* In **1607** the flight of earls occured.

=== Plantations ===
* When the irish lords were gone, king james planted ulster.
* He divided the earls land into 6 counties **Donegal, Derry, Tyrone, Fermanagh, Armagh, Cavan**.
__DDTFAC__
* He divided his estates into **1000**, **1500** and **2000 acres**.
* The estates were given two three types of **planter**.
	* **Undertaker.**
	* **Servitor.**
	* **Loyal Gaelic Irish.**
* The english brought their language, arable farming and religion.

=== County Derry ===
* County derry was given over to guilds such as the **drapers, tailors, vinters and fishmobgers**.
* This was so that they would build great towns with good buisesses.
* They also had experience setting things up, setting standards and rules and building shops.
