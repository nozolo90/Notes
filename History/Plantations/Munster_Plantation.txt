====== Munster Plantation ======


Created Sunday 03 December 2017

| Year | Organiser       | Success  |
|:-----|:----------------|:---------|
| 1584 | Queen Elizabeth | Moderate |


=== Why it happened ===
* English wanted munster land and wanted to make it more like england.
* **Fitzgerads of desmond** rebel against english
* After the english destroy them, the consficate their land.

=== What Happened ===
Queen Elizabeth split the land into **35 estates** ranging between **4,000 to 12,000 acres**.
An **undertaker** was someone who recieved an estate.
They agreed to:
	* build a castle and pay for soilders to defend it.
	* Folow protestant religion and english laws.
	* Allow english settlers, but not irish ones.
	* Build a town with a market.

=== Moderate Success ===
**Only 4000** people arrived
**Undertakers broke their agreement** and employed irish as servants and teanants.
Estates were **too large** and **hard to manage/defend**.

__Some Success__
	A few towns were succesfull eg.**killarney**
	Some industried developed, like **fishing** and **timber**.
