====== Portal Dolmens ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

**Portal dolmens** were a type of grave that Neolithic people buried their dead in

=== How They were built ===
	three upright stones were put in the ground
	a large flat stone ( called a capstone ) would be rolled on top of them using ramps made of clay

An example is **poulnabrone** dolmen, **the burren Co.Clare**
