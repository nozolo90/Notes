====== Court Cairns ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

Court cairns are **earliest type** of **megalithic tomb**

=== How they're made ===
	 upright stones would be covered in dirt and smaller stones.
	
they got their name from the open area at the entrance to the tomb (a Court)
Inside the tomb, there is a passage leading to a **chamber** in the center

=== What was in the chamber ===
	pots of cremated remains of people
	goods like **jewelry** and **weapons**

An example example is the **court cairn at creevykeel, Co.Sligo**
