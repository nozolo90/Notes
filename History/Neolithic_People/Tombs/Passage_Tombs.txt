====== Passage Tombs ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

**passage** **tombs** were like [[Court Cairns]] but much larger

passage tombs look like a large hill.
inside there is a long passage leading to a **chamber** similar to **court cairns**

An example is **newgrange Co.Meath**

=== Newgrange ===
	Newgrange is the largest passage tomb in Ireland
	people began excavating it in **1960AD**
	it was built in and around **2500BC**, making it older than **Stonehenge** and the **pyramids**
	it has a corbeled roof
	sunlight lights it up on the **21st of December** ( winter solice )
	very few people buried in it, probably only for **very important people**
