====== Religion ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

Up until the fifth century, the Celts believed in **many gods**
Druids **preached** and carried out **sacrifices **and** religious ceremonies**
You had to train 20 years to become a druid

=== Gods ===
	**An** **dagda** - main god
	**Lugh** - war god
	**Brigid** - main goddes
	**Boann** - river goddess

in the fifth century, **Christianity** was brought to ireland
**Druids** were replaced with **priests** and **monastaries** were built
