====== Sea Caves ======

Created Friday 18 August 2017

**Erosional **Landform

=== Statement ===
	* A **Sea Cave **is a tunnel or passage at the base of a cliff or through a headland

=== Development ===
	* If the rock has a weak point Destructive Waves will erode it
	* The crack gets **larger and larger** and develops into a cave

* An example is **Hook Head Co.Wicklow**
