====== Sand Spit ======

Created Friday 18 August 2017

A **Sand Spit **is a ridge of **sand** or **shingle** that extend out into the sea or across a [[The Sea:Bays and Headlands|Bay]]
its created by [[The Sea:Deposition|Deposition]]

* It begins when there is a **change of direction** in the coastline **like a bay**
* Longshore Drift moves material along the coast. **when it reaches shallow water like a bay the waves lose energy and Deposits** some material on the seabed
* Over time this material **rises above the sea level**
* It **extends across the bay** as Deposition continues

An example is **Tramore Co.Waterford**
