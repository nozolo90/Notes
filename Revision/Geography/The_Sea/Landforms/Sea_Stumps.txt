====== Sea Stumps ======

Created Friday 18 August 2017

**Erosional** landform.

=== Statement ===
* A **Sea Stump **is a stump of rock that sticks up from the seabed usually at low tide.

=== Development ===
* When the base of a [[Sea Stacks|Sea Stack]] is eroded further, it will collapse and leave a stump that is visible at low tide.

* An example is **Hook Head in County Wicklow**.
