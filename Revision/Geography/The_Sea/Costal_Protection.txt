====== Costal Protection ======
Created Friday 18 August 2017

==== Groynes ====
* These are **low walls** built of wood
* they reduce long shore drift by trapping sand.
* an example is on the **Rosslare coast**.

==== Gabions ====
* These are wire cages filled with small stones placed in front of beaches or small sand dunes.
* they reduce the power of the waves.

==== Sea Walls ====
* These are **concrete walls with** a **curve** at the top of them.
* They help to **deflect waves**.
* They can be **destroyed** in **severe storms**.

==== Boulders ====
* In this method, large boulders placed along the coast to weaken the strength of the waves before they hit the town.

All of these except the **groynes** can be found at **Tramore**
