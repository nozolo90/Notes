====== Characteristics ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

=== Characteristics of rocks are : ===
* The **Color** - the color of the rock
* The **Texture** - how the rock feels
* The **Weight** - how heavy the rock is
* The **Hardness** - how hard the rock is
* The **Density** - how dense the rock is
