====== Commuting to work ======
Created Sunday 20 May 2018

Many people live in suburbs and work in Dublin City.
People who travel to work are called **commuters**.

=== Modes of transport ===
* The most used form of transport by Dublin commuters is by **Car**.
* Followed by **bus **and **train**.

===== Traffic congestion in irish cities =====
* Rush hour traffic.
* Increased number of cars on the road.
* Increased size of the city (urban areas & population)

=== Solutions ===
* The most effective way to reduce traffic is to increase the use of public transport.
* It has been proven that just building more roads does not solve the problem.
* For people to use public transport, it must be nearby, reliable and cheap.

=== Buses ===
* If more people use buses, many cars would be taken off the road.
* Buses can carry up to 80 people.
* They can get to the city much quicker if they use **QBCs** (quality bus corridoors).
* There has been an increase in **QBCs **over the years.

=== Light rail ===
* **The Dart** which operates on the coast of Dublin. MAny people use it to commute to work each day.
* **The Luas** which is a tram system. It is a fast and efficient way of travelling.

=== Restricing car use ===
* Parking no.
* Fines
* Clamping
* Pedestrian streets.
