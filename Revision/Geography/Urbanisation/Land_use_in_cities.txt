====== Land use in cities ======
Created Sunday 20 May 2018

=== In the city ===
* High rise, high density buildings.
* Custoom House Quay in dublin.

=== In the inner city ===
* Multi storet, High desnity buildings.
* Grand Canal Street in dublin.

=== On suburbs ===
* Medium density buildings like semi detatched houses.
* Dundrum in dublin.

=== Urban fringe ===
* Low density buildings like detatched houses.
* Rathfarnham in dubin.
