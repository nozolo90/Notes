====== Variations in housing ======
Created Sunday 20 May 2018

===== Age of housing =====
* Houses tend to be older in inner city areas than in the suburbs.
* Most cities grow from the center outwards, so the oldest parts are near the center.
* An example of an old area is **York Street **in dublin. A new area is **Clonsilla** in dublin.

===== Types of housing =====
* Houses are smaller in the inner cities and larger towards the suburbs.
* Inner city homes are usually **high density**, **small terraced homes** or **apartments**.
* Many do not have gardens because of the high land values.
* Houses on the edges of the city are **low density**, **semi detatched** or **detatched** houses.
* They have gardens.

=== Public housing ===
* Some houses are built by the council or corporations.
* They are modest is size and quality.
* They are usually three bedroomed terraced or semi-detatched houses with small front and back gardens.
