====== Key Words ======


Created Saturday 19 August 2017

* **Scource** - the place where the river starts
* **Course** - the path the river takes
* **Tributary** - a small river that joins a larger river
* **Confluence** - The point where a tributary and river meet
* **Mouth** - where the river enters the sea
* **Basin** - The area of land drained by the river
* **Watershed** - the high ground separating one river basin from another
* **The Shanon** - begins in the culcadh mountains and ends in the Atlantic
* **River transportation** - Debris from upstream is carried downstream by the river
