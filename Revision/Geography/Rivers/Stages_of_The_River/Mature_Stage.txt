====== Mature Stage ======


Created Saturday 19 August 2017

**second slower and flatter**

* The river has **more water** 
* **Tributaries** join
* It flows over a **gentler slope** and [[Deposition|Deposits]] more.
* It begins to [[Rivers:Erosion|Erode]] **less vertically**(downwards) and **more laterally**(sideways)
* It **slows down**
