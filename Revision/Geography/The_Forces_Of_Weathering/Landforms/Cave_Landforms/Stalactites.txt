====== Stalactites ======

Created Saturday 19 August 2017

=== Statement ===
	* **Stalactites** are spikes **hanging down** from the roof of the cave

=== Development ===
	* drops of water containing **disolved specs of calcite ( **[[Geography:Rocks:Types Of Rock:Sedimentary:Limestone|limestone]]** )** drip down from a cave roof or evaporate , **leaving some specs behind on the roof**.
	* Over thousands of years , **theese specs build up to form stalactites**.
