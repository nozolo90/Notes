====== EarthQuakes ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

As [[Plates]] **slide past each other**, **Jagged rocks lock them in place.**
Over time pressure builds up until the rocks can no longer take it
The rocks snap releasing the pressure as [[+Shockwaves|Shockwaves]]

The place where the pressure was released is called the [[+Focus|Focus]]
The place right above the **Focus **is called the [[+Epicenter|Epicenter]]

=== Effects ===
	* Destroy buildings and property
	* Kill people

=== Ways to make damage less severe ===
	* Earthquake proof buildings
	* Wide roads
	* Roads leading away from tall buildings
