====== Airports ======
Created Saturday 12 May 2018

As air travel has become cheaper, faster, safer and more comfortable it has risen in popularity.

=== Benefits of airports ===
* Employment
* Tourist
* Stimulate business
* Stimulate light industry - light products are transported via air to nearby ind.estates.

=== Shannon Airport ===
* **Sixmilebridge and Clarecastle** have developed as dormitory towns for people working in the Shannon.
* in 1962, the town of **Shannon** was built on a greenfield site close to the airport. Many of its residents work in the airport, or industrial estates nerby
* The airport has increased tourism to cultural locations such as Limerick City.
