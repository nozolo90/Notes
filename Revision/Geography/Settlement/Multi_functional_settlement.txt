====== Multi functional settlement ======
Created Saturday 12 May 2018

=== Villages - basic ===
* Villages are usually small settlements that provide basic functions to their residents.
* They have churches, small shops, and houses.

=== Towns - more range ===
* Towns are usually larger settlements that provide large ranges of services to their residents.
* They may have cinemas, shopping centers and third level colleges.
