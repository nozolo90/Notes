====== Limerick City ======
Created Saturday 12 May 2018

Limerick City is a multi functional settlement

===== Functions =====

== Defensive ==
* Limerick began as a defensive settlement for the vikings and later, the Normans.
* The Normans built **King John's** castle and **defensive walls**.

== Market ==
* Limerick then turned into an agricultural market town.
* It now offers many **department stores** and **shopping centers**.

== Recreational ==
//Limerick has many //**cultural**// attractions like...//
* King John's castle
* The Bell Theater.
//And //**sports**// attractions like...//
* The Gaelic Grounds.
* Thodmond park.

== Educational + Residential ==
* It has 3 3_{rd} level colleges and many suburbs.
