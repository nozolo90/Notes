====== Savahna ======


Created Wednesday 06 December 2017

=== Temperature ===
* Thery are hot all year with **two seasons** (**25 to 35 degrees celcius**).

=== Precipitation ===
* They have **wet summers** and **dry winters**.
* **800ml** of precipitation annually.

=== Animals ===
* **Lions**, **cheetahs**, **girraffes**.
