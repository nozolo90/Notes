====== Sketch Maps ======
Created Saturday 20 January 2018

===== OS Maps should... =====
* Have a frame that is the correct size and drawn with a ruler.
* Have a title.
* Have a statement of scale.
* Have a coastline.
* Look nice and presentable.
* Mention north.
**Roads should be:**
	* Even width all the way through
	* drawn with two lines

===== Aerial Maps... =====
* 3D buildings should be drawn as **shaded areas**
* Have the horizon or coastline
