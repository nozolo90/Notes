====== North And South Italy ======
Created Tuesday 28 November 2017

=== North of Italy (Milan, Genoa) ===
* **Land** - There's flat ground so it's easy to build homes, roads, railway lines and factories
* **Agriculture** - There is fertile soil mud so it is easy to farm. yeoilds per hectaire are high.
* **Transport** - It is easy to build good transport links on its flat ground.
* **Industry** - This is the chief industrial regoin. 

=== South of Italy (Sicily, Sardinia) ===
* **Land** - There is Appenine Mountainous ground making it hard to build roads
* **Agriculture** - The Mountainous land, poor soil and long dry summers make farming unproductive.
* **Transport** - It is difficult and expensive to build roads and transport services on the hilly land.
* **Industry** - The lack of money or transport links discourages industry to set up.
