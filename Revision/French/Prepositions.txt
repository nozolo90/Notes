====== Prepositions ======


Created Sunday 03 September 2017

**Sur** - on
**Sous** - under

**Devant** - in front of
**Derriere** - behind

**En face de** - facing
**Dans** - in
**Avec** - with
**Sans** - without
**Entre** - in between

**Loin de** - far from
**Pres de** - close to
