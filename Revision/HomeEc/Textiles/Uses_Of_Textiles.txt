====== Uses Of Textiles ======


Created Tuesday 05 December 2017

=== Soft furnishings ===
Items other than furniture that we use to decorate a room
__Examples__
* Curtain
* Blinds
* Cushions
* Rugs

=== Carpets ===

=== Upholstrey ===
The fabric used to cover furniture like **sofas, chairs and mattresses**.

=== Bed Linen ===
Any material used on a bed
__Examples__
* Duvet / Duvet cover
* Pillow / Pillow cover
* Sheets
* Betspreads

=== Table Linen ===
Table clothes, towels, table runners, place mats.
