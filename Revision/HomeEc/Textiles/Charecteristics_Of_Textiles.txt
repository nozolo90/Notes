====== Charecteristics Of Textiles ======


Created Tuesday 05 December 2017

===== What Are They? =====
* Charecteristics of textiles are **properties** of textiles.
* **Desireable properties** are properties that are most important for a particular use.

===== Common Properties =====

=== — Fabric ===
* Stretchy
* Resiliant

=== — Resistance ===
* Crease resistant
* Fire resistant
* Shrink resistant
* Waterproof

=== — Temperature ===
* Warm
* Insulating
* Cool

=== — Cleaning ===
* Stain resistant
* Easy to clean
* Absorbant
