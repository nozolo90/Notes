====== Charecteristics Of Textiles ======


Created Tuesday 05 December 2017

===== What Are They? =====
Charecteristics of textiles are **properties** of textiles.
**Desireable characteristics/properties** are properties that are most important for a particular use.

=== Fabric ===
* Stretchy
* Resiliant

=== Resistance ===
* Crease resistant
* Fire resistant
* Waterproof
* Shrink resistant

=== Temperature ===
* Insulating
* Warm
* Cool

=== Cleaning ===
* Stain resistant
* Absorbant
* Easy to clean
