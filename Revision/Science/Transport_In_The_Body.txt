====== Transport In The Body ======


Created Friday 08 December 2017

Materials like **food**,**oxygen** and **waste** are transported to all the cells in our body via the **circulatory system**.
The circulatory system is made up of: __blood__, __blood vessels__ and __the heart the pump__.
