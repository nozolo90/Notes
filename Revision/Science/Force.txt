====== Force ======
Created Monday 21 May 2018

**Force** is measured in **newtons**.
**The moment of a force **is how much spinning motion it causes.

**Work** is measured in **joules**. It N * D.
**Power** is measured in **watts**. It is J / T.
**Pressure** is measured in **pascals**. It is N / A.
