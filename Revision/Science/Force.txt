====== Force ======
Created Monday 21 May 2018

**Force** is measured in **newtons**.
**Work** is measured in **joules**. It is force times distance.
**Power** is measured in **watts**. It is work over time.
**Pressure** is measured in **pascals**. It is force over area.
**The moment of a force **is how much spinning motion it causes.
