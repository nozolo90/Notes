====== Alkaline Metals ======
Created Monday 14 May 2018

=== Alkali metals ===
* These include **Li Na K linak**

=== Alkalines with water ===
* Alkaline metals react with water to produce <metal> hydroxide + hydrogen.
* The more protons/electrons an alkali has, the more reactive it is.

=== Alkalines react with air ===
* Alkaline metals react with air to produce <metal> oxides.
* The exposed surface become dull and not shiny.

=== Alkaline earth metals ===
* These share the same properties as alkali metals, just less extreme.
* They include **Be Mg Ca bemgca**
