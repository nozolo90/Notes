====== Developing new Materials ======
Created Thursday 03 May 2018

=== Alloys ===
* mixtures of two or more different metals

=== Plastics ===
* polymers made from crude oil extraction.
* monomers are linked together through a process called polymerisation.

=== Composites ===
* Multiple materials mixed.

=== Ceramics ===
* These include bricks and tiles. In modern times, they have been used in joint replacements an space shuttle parts.
* Non metalic synthetic mixture.
//They are//:
**— Cheap**
**— Hard**
**— Long lasting**
**— Resistant to high temperatures**
