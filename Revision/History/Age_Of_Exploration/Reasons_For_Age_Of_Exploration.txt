====== Reasons For Age Of Exploration ======
Created Friday 20 April 2018

== Demand for spices ==
* Before the age of exploration, **christian europeans** passed trade routes controlled by **muslims** whom they were often at **war** with.
* Muslims gave **high taxes** too - another disadvantage of the overland route.
* These difficulties motivated the europeans to find a **new route to asia**
__Spice Uses__
* **flavour**
* **preserving foods**

== Rulers wanted more! land and goods ==
* **rulers** wanted to gain control over **new land** to build empires.
* the new land could also contain **goods** like **gold**.
* they also wanted to **spread christianity**.
