====== Sources And Historians ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

**Sources** are pieces of evidence **Historians** use to Help find out what happened in the past
**Pre-History** is the time before writing was used
**Historic** events happen after people started writing
A **primary** **source** is a source from the event in question. eg a wittness, a diary entry, a letter, an AutoBiography
A **secondary** **source** is a source created after the event in question has taken place. They are based on primary sources. eg a history book, a Biography.
**Bias** is one sided
**Propaganda** is the spread of false information to influence other peoples opinions
