==== Farming changes ====

=== Medival system - Norfolk crop system ===
In the medival system, crops were grown on some fields and then one or two of the rest were left fallowm to gain fertility.
In the new system crops were grown on all fields, but turnips and grass were used to bring life back to the soil.
