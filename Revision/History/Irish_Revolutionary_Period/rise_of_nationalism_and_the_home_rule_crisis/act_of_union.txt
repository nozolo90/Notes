====== act of union ======
Created Thursday 20 September 2018

The act of union was passed in 1801

=== What it did ===
* A viceroy represented the king in Ireland.
* Protestantism(Anglican) became the official religion of Ireland.
* There was free trade of goods between Ireland and Britain. 
* The Irish parliament was abolished and Ireland was represented by 100 MPs in Britain.
