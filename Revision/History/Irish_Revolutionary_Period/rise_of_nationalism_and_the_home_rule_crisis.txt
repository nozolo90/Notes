====== rise of nationalism and the home rule crisis ======
Created Thursday 20 September 2018

=== Rise of nationalism ===
* In 1801, the [[+act of union|act of union]] made Ireland part of the British empire.
* Over time Irish people realised that they were not benefiting from being part of Britain.
* In 1900, most people were **nationalists**, and wanted an Irish republic.

=== Who were nationalists ===
* Catholics.
* Wanted [[+home rule|home rule]] (to take back control of Ireland.
* Felt that laws were passed to suit the British.
* Led by **John Redmond**.
