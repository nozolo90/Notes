====== Baillif ======


Created Wednesday 23 August 2017

a **ballif** ( appointed by lord ) was in charge or **enforcing** **the** **law** and **punishing** those who did wrong

=== Examples of crimes ===
* Peasants **dodging** **rent**.
* Peasants **stealling**.
* Getting **drunk** and **fighting**.

=== Possible punishments ===
* Small crimes incurred a fine.
* You could be put in stocks (socks) where your legs legs and hands were clamped in wood. Passer bys could spit at you.
* Pillories clamped your head and hands.
* You could get your hand cut off
* You could be put on a ducking stool where you would be lowered into water then brought out again and again.
* A person could be hung.
* You could be burned on a stake for witchcraft.
* Dungeons for captured soilders.
