=== People ===
	* **Abbot** - the leader of the monastary who was elected by the monks
	* almoner - looked after the poor when they visited
	* **hosteller** - looked after travelers
	* **librarian** - looked after library
	* **prior** - takes over for abbot when he is gone
	* **friars** - traveling monks eg **dominicans** , **augustinians**
	* **Benidictines** and **poor** **clares**
