====== Castles ======


Created Wednesday 23 August 2017

**Lords** would build castles on their land that they had recived from the **king**. They were built for protection.

=== Motte and Bailey ===
* Motte and Bailey castles were made from **wood** and **dirt**
* They were very **easy** and **quick** to make

== How they were built ==
* A moat would be dug arount the motte and bailey
* A **hill** would be built with the dirt from the moat to put the fort on. it was a lookout place
* The bailey was where the soilders lived

=== And Stone Castles ===
* [[+Stone Castles|Stone Castles]]

A **siege** was when the enemy surrounded a castle so no supplies could come in or out, forcing the occupants to **surrender**
