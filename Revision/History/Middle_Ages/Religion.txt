====== Religion ======


Created Wednesday 23 August 2017

Manor farms were parishes organized by priests ( who could read and write ).
Dioscese were groups of parishes ran by bishops.
Bishops built big churches called cathedrals.

=== Church Design Features ===

== Romanesque - 1000AD ==
* Rounded arches.
* Thick pillars.
* Dark interiors.

== Gothic - 1000AD+ ==
* Thin pillars
* Pointed, triangular arches
* Beautiful designs on tinted glass windows called rose windows.
* Flying buttreses were exterior supports.
* Spires were the tall spikes.
