====== Craftsmen ======


Created Wednesday 23 August 2017

**Craftsmen** were people who made things that other people needed
for example, **bakers**,**smiths**,**fishers** and **butchers** are craftsmen

===== Becoming a mastercraftsman takes three stages =====
	

== 1. Apprentices ==
* At **12 **years old the boy will live with a **master craftsman** and learn the trade

== 2. Journeymen ==
* At **19** years old the boy was now a **journeyman**.
* He could get **paid** for his work and look **elsewhere** **for** **jobs**

== 3. Mastercraftsman ==
* When a **journeyman** creates a **masterpiece** , he advances to this stage.
* A **masterpiece **is a product a **journeyman** produces that the guild decides is **very high quality**
* He can have his own **workshop** and sell his own **goods**.
* To get into a [[+Guilds|guild]] ,  he would have to pay a large sum of money
* **Craftsmen **are part of organisations called **guilds**
