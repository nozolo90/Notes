====== Lord & Lady ======


Created Wednesday 23 August 2017

=== Education ===
* Most lords or ladies did not read or write, as they had a priest to do it for them.
* The priest taught the Lord's son until he was 7 years old.

=== The Lady ===
* The lady was the **wife** of the lord
* Her parents would give the lords family a **dowry** ( money or goods ) in exchange for the man to take care of her

=== Jobs of the Lady ===
* learned how to do **household** **work**
* **cured** **illnesses** with **herbs**
* did **embroidery**
* when the lord was away, they would take care of the castle

=== Food ===
* Rich people ate alot of **meat** (**duck** , **rabbit** , **hare** , **lamb** , **pork** , **boar** , **birds**)
* The most important people sat at the **top** **tables**
* food was served on plates or slices of bread called **trenchers**
* They drank **ale**

=== Clothes ===
* Women wore long dresses that trailed off on the floor made of **wool** & **silk** + a headdress called a **wimple**
* Men wore **long** **tunics** and **pointed** **leather** **shoes**.


=== Entertainment ===
* **Hawking** - hunting with trained birds
* **Tournaments** - knights fought mock battles with each other
* **Jousting** - Knights charge at each other with blunt swords called lances
* **minstrels** - singers
* **jesters** - clowns
