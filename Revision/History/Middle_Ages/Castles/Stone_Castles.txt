====== Stone Castles ======


Created Wednesday 23 August 2017

**Stone Castles **were stronger than **motte and bailey castles**

=== Buildings ===
	* [[+Keep|keep]] - lord & lady lived here - very strong (thick walls) - narrow windows good defence
	* **ramparts** - paths on top of the walls
	* **baws**/**bailey**/**courtyard** - soilders lived here
	* **stables** - knights horses
	* **kitchens** - food was made by cooks
	* **forge** - smith worked here -  horseshoes were made
	* **well** - fresh water supply without leaving castle
	* **latrines** - toilets
	* **battlements** - on top of keep soilders were put here to look for enemies

=== Protection ===
	* **curtain** **walls** - walls you can peep out of, attack then cover
	* **Turrets** - strong towers along the walls
	* **Drawbridge** - raised when enemies were near
	* **Portcuillis** - Iron grille - like a door
	* **moat** - ditch around the castle making it less accesible to the enemy
