====== Peasants ======


Created Tuesday 22 August 2017

Peasants farmed on the knights land with the [[Open Field System]]
They gave some of their produce to the church
This was called **Tithe**

=== There were two types of peasant ===
	[[+Serfs|Serfs]]
	[[+Freemen|Freemen]]


=== Food ===
	**Peasants** ate **bread,** **ale** ( safer than water ) **cheese** and **veg** **soup** called **pottage**


=== Clothes ===
	* Peasants made their own cothes out of **linen** or **wool**.
	* They **dyed** their clothes with **berries** or **mosses**
	* Men wore **tunics**
	* Women wore **dresses** with a **headscarf**

=== Passtimes ===
	* noughts and crosses
	* wrestling
	* draughts

=== Houses ===
	Peasants lived in [[History:Wattle and Daub|Wattle and Daub]] houses with **thatched** **straw** **roofs**
	In the cold weather , amnimals would come in too
