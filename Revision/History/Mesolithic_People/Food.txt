====== Food ======


Created Sunday 20 August 2017

The Mesolithic people were **hunter gatherers**
this means that they hunted for meat and gathered other foods.

=== They hunted... ===
* fish, wild boar, deer

=== They gathered... ===
* nuts, berries

they were **nomadic **so they traveled a lot. a nomad is a traveler
The food pieces fount at [[Mount Sandel]] show us that the Mesolithic people had a varied diet
