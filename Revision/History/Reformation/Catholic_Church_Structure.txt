====== Catholic Church Structure ======


Created Wednesday 29 November 2017

* The **pope** **ruled the catholic church** and central italy
* **Cardinals helped** the pope
* **Dioceses** were like counties ruled by a bishop
* **Parishes** were found in dioceses, each with one or more priests
* People who lived in the parish payed the **tithe**
* There were also **monastaries** and **convents**
