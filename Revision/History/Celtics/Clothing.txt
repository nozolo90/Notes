====== Clothing ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

=== Celtic men ===
* Rich men wore knee length tunics called and belts called crois.
* Broke niggas wore trousers.

=== Celtic women ===
* Rich women wore ankle length tunics called brats.
* Poor women wore long tunics.

In winter men and women would wear **woolen cloaks pined at the shoulder with a gold or silver broch**.

The Celts wore [[History:Bronze Age:Torcs & Lunlaes|Torcs & Lunlaes]]
The Celts dyed their hair with a mixture of lime and water called **Woad**

**Druids** ( priests ) wore **linen** **tunics**
