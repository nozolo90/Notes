====== Farming ======


Created Monday 21 August 2017

**Farming **began in a middle eastern place called Mesopotamia
**Farming** made people rely less on **hunting and gathering** to get food
People began to build more permanent houses and settlements close to farms

=== What they farmed ===
	Wheat
	Barley
	Sheep
	Cattle

**Neolithic** **people** built large and long stone walls to protect their animals from going astray and being attacked
An example is **Ceide fields, Co Mayo** ( farming stopped in **3000BC** )
