====== Volcanoes ======


Created Tuesday 22 August 2017

In **79AD** mount **vesuvius** erupted

the city of **pomepei** in a layer of ash and dust **4 meters** deep
the city of **herculaleum** was covered in layers of hot ash **25 meters deep**

the ash preserved everything in the towns very well

**Archaeologists** began [[History:Archeology:Excavations|Excavating]] pompei in **1970AD**

=== What was found ===
* paintings
* furniture
* householed shrines
* uneaten meals
* preserved human bodies
* jewlery
* coins
* statues
