====== Stages Of The Roman Empire ======


Created Tuesday 22 August 2017

=== Kingdom Phase ===
* In **460BC**, Rome was a **kingdom** ruled by a king.
* The people couldn't vote.

=== Republic Phase ===
* The people were not happy and in **510BC **there was a revolution which led to the creation of the **republic**.
* People could vote on the **senate**
* the **senate** voted on two **consuls **who would rule Rome.

=== Empire Phase ===
* When Rome began conquering land, it became an **Empire**(a country that controls many land).
* **Governors **ruled provences, roman countries.
* An **Emperor **ruled Rome (**Hadrian,  Augustus Ceaser, Nero, Caligula**).

=== The Fall ===
* **Barbaric** **tribes** such as the **Vandals** and **Francs** lived on the outskirts of the roman empire.
* In around **400AD**, they poured into rome, **looking** **for** **land** and **stealling** **treasures**.
* Half of the roman empire was now **destroyed**, but the other half ( called the **byzantine empire** ) would live on
