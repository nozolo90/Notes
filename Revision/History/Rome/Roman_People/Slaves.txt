====== Slaves ======


Created Tuesday 22 August 2017

=== Who were they ===
* Slaves belonged to their masters and had no rights. They received no pay and had no say in what they did.
* They made up **one third** of the **population**.

=== How they became slaves ===
* They were usually criminals, captured enemy citizens or people sold into slavery.

=== Work ===
* Some were educated and worked as teachers, doctors or tutors.
* Some were forced to fight as gladiators and be chariot racers.
* Some worked with patritians in their homes.
