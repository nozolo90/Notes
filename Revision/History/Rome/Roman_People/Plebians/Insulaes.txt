====== Insulaes ======


Created Tuesday 22 August 2017

Insulaes were **tall wooden flats**.
They were around **five stories tall**, with **shops in the first story**
**whole families** were crammed into **one** or **two rooms**
**water was only piped to the bottom story**, so you needed to carry **buckets of water** upstairs
**fires were common** due to them being built with **cheap wood**
