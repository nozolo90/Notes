====== Our Constitution ======
Created Thursday 10 May 2018

Our constitution is called Bunreacht na hEireann (Constitution of Ireland).
It was created in 1937.

=== Articles ===
The Constitution is split into 50 articles which all describe the many aspects of our country,
//For example//
* Article 12 to Article 14 describe the role of the president.
* Article 15 to Article 28A describe how the government is organized and what role each part of it has.
* Article 40 to Article 44 describe the rights that people have.
* Article 46 to Article 50 describe how the constitution can be amended through a referendum.
