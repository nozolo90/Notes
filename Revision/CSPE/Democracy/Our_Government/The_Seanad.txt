====== The Seanad ======
Created Thursday 10 May 2018

The Seanad/Senate is a group of 60 senators which debate over bills and issues.
The Seanad works with the Dail to introduce new bills.
They usually sit on tuesdays and wednesdays.

=== Senators ===
* If a senator is a member of a political party they may attend meetings with ministers and TDs to agree with their agendas.
* Like TDs, they may do constituency work.
