====== Animals ======
Created Wednesday 09 May 2018

**Animal welfare** - looking after the living conditions of animals
**Animal rights** - issues such as experimenting on animals, hurting animals and breeding animals forcefully.

=== How animals are threatened: ===
* People destroy their habitat to build on.
* People hunt or poach them.
* People hunt them for their husks.
* They are killed by disease.

=== Endangered animals ===
* Dodo bird
* Panda
* South African Elephant.
* Blue Whale
* Polar Bear.
