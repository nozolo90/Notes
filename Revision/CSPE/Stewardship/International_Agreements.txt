====== International Agreements ======
Created Wednesday 09 May 2018

=== The kyoto protocol ===
* This was an agreement between countries to reduce their greenhouse gas emmisions.
* Ireland signed it in 2002, America has not.
* This shows that most developed countries are willing to make changes in order to protect the environment.

=== Copenhagen accord ===
* This states that Climate change is an ongoing issue and countries must take efforts t keep temperature increases below 2 degrreessss celcius.
* Unfourtunately, it is not legaly binding.

=== EU change campaign ===
* Passes laws to protect the environment.
* Provides grants for environmental projects.
* Organizes conferences and meetings; set up by the EEPA.
