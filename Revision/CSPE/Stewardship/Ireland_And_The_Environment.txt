====== Ireland And The Environment ======
Created Wednesday 09 May 2018

=== How Ireland has taken climate action ===
* Plastic bag levy (2002)
* WEEE introduced in 2005 to ensure the correct disposal of electronics.
* Water and Litter pollution acts (1997)
* 40% of vehicles to be electric by 2030.
* Power One campaign.
//These changes are though up by the Department of Environment, Heritage and Local Government.//

=== Campaigns against bad ===
* Earth hour - the lights of famous buildings are turned off for one hour.
* Earth Day - 222nd of April.
