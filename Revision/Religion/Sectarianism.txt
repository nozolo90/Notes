====== Sectarianism ======


Created Thursday 24 August 2017

**Sectarianism **is the hatred of people because they belong to a different religious group

**Religious conflict** is clash or struggle between different religious groups

Religious conflict gets worse when there is sectarianism

=== Example ===
* **protestants** and **christians** in **northern** **ireland**
