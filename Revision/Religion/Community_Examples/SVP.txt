====== SVP ======


Created Thursday 24 August 2017

	{{../../../../Images/saint_vincent_de_paul_logo.jpeg?width=273}}


**Saint Vincent de Paul** is a community of faith named after a famous french saint who helped the poor
It was founded by **Frederic** **Ozanam** in **Paris**
Frederic ozanam was inspired by jesus's teaching in the gospel to //"love god and love ones neighbour as yourself "//

=== They help the poor through: ===
	* Money management
	* Hospital visits
	* Homework clubs
	* Creches
	* Home  visits

SVP works in over **100** **countries**
