=== Foundation ===
* **Saint Vincent de Paul** was founded by **Frederic** **Ozanam** in **Paris**.
* Frederic Ozanam was inspired by Jesus's teaching in the gospel to //"love god and love ones neighbor as yourself "//.
* It is named after a famous french saint of the same name who helped the poor.

=== Purpose ===
* People can volunteer to give 2 or 3 hours a week to help the poor and the needy.
