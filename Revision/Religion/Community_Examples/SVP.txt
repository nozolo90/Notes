=== Founder ===
* **Saint Vincent de Paul** is a community of faith named after a famous French saint who helped the poor.
* It was founded by **Frederic** **Ozanam** in **Paris**.
* Frederic Ozanam was inspired by Jesus's teaching in the gospel to //"love god and love ones neighbor as yourself "//.

=== Purpose ===
* People can volunteer to give 2 or 3 hours a week to help those in need.
