====== Arrest To Pentecost ======


Created Sunday 10 December 2017

=== Arrest and Trial - Thursday ===
* Jesus was in the **garden of gethsemane** to pray with his disciples
* He knew he would soon be arrested by the romans
* Suddenly **Judas and many roman gaurds** come **appear to arrest jesus**.
* **Judas kisses Jesus** to signal the roman gaurds.
* The soilders seize Jesus and **he is brought to the sanhedrin to be tried**.
* The high priest Caphias accuses him of blasphemy and **scentences him to death**.
* The sanhedrin didn't have the power to kill Jesus so they bring him to the roman emporer **puntious pilate**.
* The people have to chose between letting Jesus go, or a convicted criminal Barabus.
* The crowd chooses Barabus and to avoid a riot, **Jesus is scentenced to death**.

=== Death - Friday ===
* Jesus carries is forced to carry a cross up **golgotha**.
* He falls 3 times and is helped by simon.
* **He is nailed to the cross at the top** along with two other criminals.
* One mocks him and the other asks for forgiveness
* After **6 hours** on the cross, he dies.
* His last words were //"Father, in your hands I place my spirit".//
* Christians see Jesus' death as a sarcrifice. He sarcrificed himself to save us from sin.

=== Resurection - 3 days after ===
* Mary Magdalene, Joanna and Mary go to the tomb of Jesus to wash and enbalm his body.
* They arrive at the tomb and the rock has been rolled away.
* Jesus and the gaurds were not there.
* A messenger from God tells them //"Jesus is not here, he is risen from the dead. Don't be afraid "//
* The women run back to tell the disciples - they do not believe them.
* Peter the disciple goes to the tomb and is surprised to see that they were telling the truth.
* Jesus appeared to many people after the resurection.
* He had transformed and the disciples didn't recognise them at first.

=== Ascension - Sunday 40 days later ===
* Jesus brings his disciples out into Emmaus.
* In Emmaus, Jesus accends into heaven.
