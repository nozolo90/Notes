====== Miracles ======
Created Thursday 24 May 2018

[[Palestine:Map|Where he did miracles]]

=== Nature miracles ===
* This is when Jesus affected something in nature.
* An example is when he calmed the raging sea.

=== Healing miracles ===
* This is when Jesus healed somebody from diease or illness.
* When he cured the blind man, paralyzed man and lepper.

=== Raising to life miracles ===
* When Jesus raised people to life again.
* When Jesus raised Lazarus's to life in bethanee.

=== Expelling spirit miracles ===
* When Jesus expelled evil spirits from people.
* When Jesus expelled the evil spirit from the boy in Capernaum.
