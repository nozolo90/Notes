====== Early Faith Communities ======


Created Sunday 10 December 2017

The first Christian community began in jerusalem
The dicsiples **taught the word of God**, **baptised new belivers** and **gave the euchairist**.

=== Way of life ===
* __Faith:__ They were baptised and believed in the risen lord
* __Worship:__ They met and prayed in the temples
* __:__ They lived based on the teachings of jesus.

=== Problems ===
* Sadduces feared the popularity of the group and began **torturing and imprisoning** those involved.
* The members of the church were forced to meet in secret.
* **Others fled jerusalem** safely.

=== St Stephen ===
* St stephen was the first to be arrested and brought before the sanhedrin.
* He was then scentenced to death after being accused of **blasphemy**.
* He became the **first Christian maytr**.
