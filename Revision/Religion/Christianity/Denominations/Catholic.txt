====== Catholic ======


Created Thursday 24 August 2017


Catholics leader is the Pope in Rome

== Hierarchy ==
	**Lay people** - baptised catholics
	**Priests** - leader of the parish - do mass and sarcraments
	**Bishops** - leader of a diocese ( group of parishes )
	**Arch Bishops** - senior bishop
	**Cardinals** - give advice to the pope in important matters
	**Pope** - leader of the catholic church
