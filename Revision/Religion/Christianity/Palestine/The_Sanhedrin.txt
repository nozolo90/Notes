====== The Sanhedrin ======
Created Sunday 20 May 2018

=== Details ===
* The Sanhedrin is the **Jewish Court of law**.
* It is composed of **70** **Pharisees/Sadducee** and **1 high priest**.
* They did not have the power to execute.
* They are located in **Jerusalem**.
