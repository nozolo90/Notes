====== Stages Of  The hajj ======


Created Tuesday 12 December 2017

=== 1. Mecca ===
* Muslims ritualy wash themselves.
* Men shave their beards
* They change into white clothes.
	* This shows that they are all equal in the presence of Allah.

=== 2. The Ka'ba ===
* The Ka'Ba is the most sacred shrine of islam
* Muslims arrive at mecca and circle the Ka'ba 7 times.
* There is a black rock which muslims bellieve allah sent from heaven
* Muslims drink water from the holy well called zamzam.

=== 3. Hills of safa and mara ===
* Muslims walk or run between these two hills 7 times
* In the past this was outside but now it is in a tunnel.

=== 4. Plain of arafat ===
* This plane is 24 killometers away from mecca
* It is where muhamed delivered his last sermon.
* Muslims spend a day there praying and praying for forgivness.

=== 5. Pillars of mina ===
* Muslims throw stones at these pillars.
* This reminds them of when abraham threw stones at the devil to ward him away.
* Muslims then return to the kaba and circle it 7 more times
