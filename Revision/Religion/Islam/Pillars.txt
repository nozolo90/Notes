====== Pillars ======
Created Sunday 10 September 2017

{{../../../../Images/5_pillars_of_islam.jpeg?width=300}}

===== The five pillars are the moral code for islam. =====

=== Belief - Shahadah ===
* Muslims believe in one god, allah.
* There is no god but allah and mohamed is the prophet of allah
* This is the creed, a summarry of a religions mos deeply held beliefs

=== Prayer - Salat ===
* Mulims must pray to allah **5** **times** **a** **day**
* on friday they play in the **mosque**

=== Charity - Zakat ===
* Give **2.5%** of their savings to help those in need

=== Fasting - Sawm ===
* Muslims must fast from **sunrise** to **sunset** during the month of **ramadah**
* during **ramadan** muslims also **pray** **more** **often**,read more of the **qu'ran** and **give more to charity**
* during ramadan, muslims also pray more and read more of the Qu'ran.

=== Pilgramage - Hajj ===
* Muslims must make atleast one journey to the ka'ba in their life time
* The ka'ba is in mecca, the birthplace of muhamed and it is the most **sacred** **shrine** **of** **islam**
