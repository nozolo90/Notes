====== Muslim Festivals ======


Created Tuesday 12 December 2017

===== Ramadan =====
* this is the **holiest month** in Islam.
* It **reminds muslims** of when angel gabriel revealed himself to Allahh, on the night of power and exelence.
* during ramadan, **Muslims fast** from **sunrise to sunset**.
* Fasting is a sign that religion is more important than everything.
__During ramadan:__
	* Muslims pray more.
	* Read more of the Qu'ran
	* Give more to charity

===== Eid ul-fitr =====
* **3 day festival** at the end of ramadan.
* Joyful festival to **thank Allah for a succesful fast**.
* on the 1_{st }day muslims pray and give thanks.
* Muslims say **eid mubark**, meaning **blessing and joy** of eid.
* Homes celebrate with decorations, cakes, gifts and presents.
* Mulims go to the muslim cemetary to pray for the dead.

===== Du'al hijjah =====
* this is when the **hajj begins**.
* Reminds muslims of when Abraham was ready to sacrifice his son but Allah gave him an animal instead.
* The festival **begins and ends in mecca**.

===== Eid ul-adah =====
* This festival takes place **two months after eid ul fitr**, at the **end of the hajj pilgramage**.
* The sarcrifice made to Allah by abraham is a sign that muslims are ready to give up everything for Allah.
* A portion of a saarcrifice is given to the poor.
* Gifts and cards are exchanged during the three day festival.
