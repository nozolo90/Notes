====== Mosques ======


Created Sunday 10 December 2017

=== Statement ===
* A mosque is a muslims place of worship. It is a building where people go to pray and worship allah.

== More ==
	* The word mosque means a place where people bow down in the precence of God.
	* Some mosques have domes on them. They represent the universe which Allah has control.
	* Mosques are mostly bare. Decorated lines from the Qu'ran are displayed on the walls.

=== Imam ===
* The imam is a man who people believe follows the rules of the Qu'ran and knows it welll.
* He stands at the minibar

=== Wudu ===
* Wudu is a ritual muslims do to prepare for prayer.
* Muslims wash their feet,arms, hands and head to appear clean and pure to Allah.
* Some mosuqes have a wudu area where muslims can do the ritual.

=== Minaret ===
* A minaret is a tall narrow tower that a mezzuzin stands on to call muslims to prayer.
* In modern times, we use a speaker instead of having the mezzuzin climb up.
* A mezzuzin is a crier who calls muslims to prayer.

=== Mihrab ===
* A mihrab is an alcove in a mosque pointing towards mecca.
* The imam (prayer leader) stands in it and performs prayer rituals facing into the alcove towards mecca.
