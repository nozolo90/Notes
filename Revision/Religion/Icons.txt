====== Icons ======
Created Wednesday 21 March 2018

An icon is a **holy image** of Jesus, Mary or one of the Saints.

* They are heavily used in Orthodox churches
* Christians Don't Pray to icons, they are only to help them focus.

=== Iconographer ===
* The Iconographer is the person who creates the icons.
* The creation of an icon is seen as a very spiritual process.
* The iconographer may meditate or t is get confession brfore painting.
* The icon is not meant to be an extremely realistic depiction.
* Certian parts are exagerated, for example the head and fingers.

=== Iconostasis ===
* This is a solid screen that separates the sanctuary from the area that the congregation gather to worship.
* It is a symbol that  represents the separation  between heaven and earth.
* The iconostasis is usually decorated with icons.
* The word iconostasis means the place od icons or pictures.
