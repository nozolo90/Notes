====== Inter Faith Dialogue ======


Created Thursday 24 August 2017

**Inter Faith Dialogue **is open and honest discusions between different religious groups.

=== Examples ===
* The three faiths.
* council of **Christians** and **Jews**.


=== Why Inter Faith Dialogue is important ===
* To promote tolerance and understanding between members of different world religions
* To promote peace between different world religions.
