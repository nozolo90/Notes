=== Aspects of a good opening ===
* An interesting point of view (1st person, past, present).
* Interesting characters introduced (contrasting, unique).
* Interesting character relations (freinds, enemies, family, meeting each other).
* Interesting setting (vivid description).
* Giving the reader questions that they want answered (mystery).

=== Aspects of a good ending ===
* Story comes full circle.
* An unpredictable element
